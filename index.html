Welcome to the Wild Animal Mini-Zoo!

On this page, you'll find information about our animals and their needs.

Bears

  • Ollie
  • Mona

Ollie

Ollie is a grizzly bear who loves to swim and play with his toys. He needs a lot of space to move around, and he eats mainly fish and berries.

Learn more about grizzly bears on Wikipedia

Mona

Mona is a black bear who likes to climb trees and take long naps. She is a herbivore and eats mostly plants and fruits.

Learn more about black bears on Wikipedia

Giraffes

  • Frankie
  • Coconut

Frankie

Frankie is a Masai giraffe who likes to eat leaves from tall trees. He can grow up to 18 feet tall and his long neck helps him reach his food.

Learn more about giraffes on Wikipedia

Coconut

Coconut is a reticulated giraffe who likes to socialize with other giraffes. She eats leaves and fruits, and her spots are unique to her.

Learn more about reticulated giraffes on Wikipedia

Lions

  • Mella
  • Karl

Mella

Mella is a female African lion who likes to hunt in groups. She eats meat and needs a lot of exercise to stay healthy.

Karl

Karl is a male Asian lion who likes to rest in the shade. He eats meat and needs a balanced diet to stay healthy.
